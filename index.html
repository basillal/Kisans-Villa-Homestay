Who We Are

A Sanctuary for Families & Friends

Kisans Villa Homestay sits in the quiet green village of Varadimoola, Mananthavady. It is a cozy private villa where families and friends can relax, enjoy good food, and feel at home in nature.

We are just a short drive from Mananthavady town, but calm and peaceful enough to truly unwind. It is the perfect base to explore the beauty of Wayanad.
